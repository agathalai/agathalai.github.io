Logging in for the first time

In order to log in and access the Cmd web interface, you must receive an email invitation from the system. The email invitation will prompt you to log in and set a new password.

NOTE: If you have not received an email invitation, check your junk mail. If the email invitation cannot be found, ask your system administrator to send you a new invitation.

  1. Click the link in the email to assign a new password to your account.

  2. After you set a new password, you will be prompted to log in.
    • Enter your email and the new password, then click Login.
    • If you are using your Google account email, you can click Login with Google.

      Tip: Even though you can log in using Google, the password you set is still required to verify certain configurations within the system. Don't forget your password.

  3. If you have access to more than one project, you will be prompted to select one to continue.

Once logged in, you will see the Home page. Continue setting up your account based on your role in the system.
