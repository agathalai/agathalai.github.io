Change password

The Change password page allows you to change your account password.

  1. In the top-right corner, click .

    The My account page is displayed.

  2. From the left menu pane, click Change password.

    If you do not see the left menu pane, swipe to the left browser edge to show the menu.

  3. On the following page, enter your current password then enter your new password twice.
  4. Click Change password to save your new password.
