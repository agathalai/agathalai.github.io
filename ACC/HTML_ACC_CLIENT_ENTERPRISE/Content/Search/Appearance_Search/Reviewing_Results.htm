Reviewing Search Results

By default, the search results are sorted by Relevance. The thumbnails are displayed in decreasing confidence for each time interval.

In the top-left area, you can click the Camera toggle to display the results by camera. In this view, the search results are grouped by camera. Search results are displayed in decreasing confidence from left to right for each camera.

Reviewing the search results allows you to determine if the result contains relevant information.

The search results populated on the screen may not always show an exact match of the person or vehicle you are looking for. For example, the thumbnails may show other people with similar clothing, or different vehicle models of the same color.

  • Use the search results graph to quickly identify similar results over a larger time frame.

    • The yellow star identifies the original search object.

      More stars are added as you refine the search results and include additional reference images. For more information, see Refining Search Results.

    • Peaks in the graph represent objects that are similar to the original search object.
    • Move the light blue handles to change the search time range.

      The search results update to show video from that time.

    • Use your mouse scroll wheel to zoom in and out of the graph.
  • Click on a search result to review the video and confirm that it relates to the object of the search.

    The video associated with the search result is displayed in the image panel on the right.

  • To zoom in on the area of interest, hover over the search result and click or double-click the result thumbnail.

    The video associated with the search result is displayed in the image panel, and is zoomed into the area of interest.

  • To play the video associated with the search result, click in the image panel.
  • Refer to the left search information panel to compare the search results with the object of your search.

    The panel displays a Full Profile reference image. It also displays Face Profile image if the original image or starred results provide a clear reference image.

    In the top-left corner, click to show or hide the search information panel.

After you have reviewed the search results, you can refine the search to find more accurate results. For more information, see Refining Search Results.
