1 Regulations and Guidelines

The SGO project falls under the jurisdictions of the Government of Canada and the Province of Saskatchewan. The Saskatchewan Ministry of Environment regulates the permitting, design, construction, operation, maintenance, surveillance, and closure of the TMFs.

The table below provides a list of licence requirements and guidelines that govern the structure and content of this manual.

Reference

Regulation or Guideline

Relevance

CDA 2013

Dam Safety Guidelines (2007 revised in 2013).

Guidance related to design and operation of dams.

CDA 2014

Technical Bulletin: Application of Dam Safety Guidelines to Mining Dams

Guidance related to design, operation, and closure of tailings dams.
CDA 2019 Revision to the Environmental Consequence Classification Procedures for Mining Dams. Revision to be inserted as an appendix to Technical Bulletin: Application of Dam Safety Guidelines to Mining Dams.

Addresses the main mechanisms of environmental impact from a mining dam failure (damage to habitats).
Revision to Section 2.4 of the Technical Bulletin: Application of Dam Safety Guidelines to Mining Dams.

This revision provides clarification on what is meant by a landform and a mine waste structure.
Revision to Section 3.7.4.1 of the Technical Bulletin: Application of Dam Safety Guidelines to Mining Dams.

This section deals with the target factor of safety for mining dams.

Revision to Section 3.1 of the Technical Bulletin: Application of Dam Safety Guidelines to Mining Dams.

This revision addresses dam safety management and the EOR.

ICOLD 2003

Dam Surveillance Guide – Bulletin 158

Guidance specific to dam surveillance.

MAC 2011

A Guide to Audit and Assessment of Tailings Facility Management

Guidance for audit and inspection of tailings facilities.

SK MOE 2019

Approval to Operate Pollutant Control Facilities (Approval No. PO19-193)

Under the authority of the General Manager.

BCWMB 2016

Dam Safety Guidelines – Inspection & Maintenance of Dams

Dam safety guidance related to inspection & maintenance of dams (Useful Visuals).

GTR 2020GT

Global Industry Standard on Tailings Management

The Standard provides a framework for safe tailings facility management while affording Operators flexibility as to how best to achieve this goal (GTR 2020).

MAC 2021a

A Guide to the Management of Tailings Facilities (Version 3.2)

Guidance for management and operation of tailings facilities.

MAC 2021b

Developing an Operation, Maintenance and Surveillance Manual for Tailings and Water Management Facilities (Version 2.1)

Guidance for structure and content of tailings OMS manuals.
