RASCI Matrix

Within the DSMS framework, the owner should identify the type of involvement for each position and function to form a customized RASCI matrix. The type of involvement is defined as follows (based on ICOLD Bulletin 154 and CDA, 2019):

  • Responsible (R): Position that performs the functional activity. The responsibility may be shared and/or delegated. But, the sharing and/or delegation must be clearly assigned and does not change the overall responsibility of the position. Hence the shared and delegation is typically not shown.
  • Accountable (A): Ultimately accountable for the functional activity and has veto power over it. Accountable for completion of the task and review the results. This position ensure that the functional activity is completed.
  • Support (S): Position that provides support during the implementation of the functional activity. Allocates resources and assists the Responsible person to complete the activity.
  • Consulted (C): Position that is consulted prior to or during the task. The position brings expertise to the task or could be affected by the execution and/or completion of the task. This may include subject matter experts.
  • Informed (I): Position that is informed of key aspects of the process, including progress, status and results. Typically, after a decision or action is taken (otherwise, this could be confused with the Consulted role). Information to be provided should be clearly defined.
