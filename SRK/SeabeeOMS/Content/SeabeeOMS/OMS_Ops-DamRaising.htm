East Lake TMF

Construction stages 1 to 4 of the East Lake TMF expansion to the interim crest elevation have been completed. One construction stage remains. All construction phases have or will be completed in accordance with issued for construction specifications and drawings (SRK 2015f). Further construction on the East Lake Expansion Dike is delayed until downstream stabilization efforts are complete.

Note:

  1. The pond elevation within the East Lake TMF basin shall be managed as low as practicable.
  2. The proposed "max" pond elevations provided in the Operations Plan (SRK 2015d) are designed to reduce seepage. They are based on the anticipated tailings configuration shown for a given year but will depend on the actual tailings configuration achieved in the field.
  3. Operational criteria for the area upstream of the Expansion Dike require that the elevation of the tailings and supernatant pond must be maintained at a minimum of 0.5 m below the lowest constructed crest elevation of the Expansion Dike.
  4. The ultimate operational criteria require that the water level downstream of the Expansion Dike (by the seepage collection pumps and Back Pond) must be maintained below the current max operating level of 459.5 m elevation.

Triangle Lake TMF

The Triangle Lake TMF has been constructed to an interim permitted crest elevation of 463.0 m (Phase 1 expansion). The Phase 2 expansion will commence in Q3 of 2020 and be completed in Q4 of 2021. The Phase 2 expansion incorporates construction of Portage South Dam, Laonil Dam, and Quarry Dam to a final permitted crest elevation of 466.0 m. This manual will need to be revised following this expansion.
