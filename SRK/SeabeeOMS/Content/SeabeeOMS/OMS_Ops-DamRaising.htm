All construction phases have or will be completed in accordance with issued for construction specifications and drawings (SRK 2015f). Further construction on the EL Expansion Dike is delayed (max 1 m lift to elev. 466 m) until downstream stabilization efforts are complete.

3.2 Triangle Lake TMF

The TL TMF has been constructed and permitted to a crest elevation of 466.0 m (Phase 2 expansion, governed by the crest elevation of the exterior dams).

The Phase 2 expansion construction was complete in October 2021. The Phase 2 expansion included construction of Portage South Dam, Portage West Dam, Laonil Dam, and Quarry Dam to a final crest elevation of 466.0 m. Once permitted, final configuration of the TL TMF (combined Phases 1 and 2 basins) will provide containment of supernatant water to a FSL elevation of 464.2 m, which accommodates temporary storage of the IDF and 1.1 m of required freeboard.

As described in Water Management, the TL TMF will continue to operate as two separate basins (divided by the South Dam) until 2027. Non-contact water in the Phase 2 basin will be discharged to the environment until deposition commences in TL Phase 2 basin in mid December 2024. The Phase 2 TL TMF basin is like the EL TMF and is to be operated as dry as practicable.
