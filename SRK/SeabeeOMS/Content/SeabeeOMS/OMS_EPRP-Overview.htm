Overview

This section lists the emergency preparedness and response plan (EPRP) specific to the tailings management facilities. It is therefore the reference section for all emergencies related to the TMFs. Other documents such as the Seabee site-wide emergency preparedness plan (EPP) and emergency response plan (ERP) should reference this section.

The current ERP for the SGO (SSR 2019) does not include a section dedicated to the TMFs.

Objectives

The goals of an EPRP are to prevent the occurrence of emergencies and to reduce the impact of emergencies should they arise. These goals are to protect human life and health, social well-being of the local community and employee, the environment, and the facilities at the Seabee Mine. To meet these goals, the objectives of the tailings facility EPRP are to ensure

  • timely and appropriate response to emergency situations; and
  • compliance with applicable laws, industry standards, and legal codes of practice.

Scope

Emergencies at either tailings facility may include the following:

  • Failure of any structure that retains tailings;
  • Major cracking, sliding, or bulging of any tailings-retaining structure;
  • Rapid increase in seepage through a dam, or dike, or their foundations;
  • formation of a sinkhole on a dam or dike; and
  • major storm event or flood that results in overtopping.

The EPRP has been developed specifically for the EL and TL TMFs and should be read in conjunction with the TARPs (Appendix B). The EPRP outlines the steps required by TARPs for each operating parameter. Actions will vary depending on the TARP level.

An orange level will trigger the application of the corrective actions listed in the emergency preparedness plan (EPP), which lists potential credible threats and the corresponding corrective actions. A red or critical-condition level will trigger the application of the reactive actions listed in the emergency response plan (ERP), which lists potential failure modes and corresponding reactive actions and tasks that imply imminent failure or that failure has occurred.

This tailings EPRP also includes reporting duties and applicable action for each TARP level, tailings EPRP reporting procedure (Appendix D), and a description of the Crisis Management Team.
