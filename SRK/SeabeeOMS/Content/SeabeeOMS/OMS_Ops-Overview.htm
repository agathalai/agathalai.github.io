Objective

Operation of the TMFs at the Seabee Mine primarily involves ongoing construction at the TMFs, deposition of slurried tailings into two facilities, and managing supernatant water levels. The following operational flowchart, recommended by the Mining Association of Canada (MAC 2011), provides a general overview of the sequence of operational steps. This framework corresponds to the “Plan-Do-Check-Act" management protocol.

This manual will be updated to reflect future changes to the tailings deposition and water management procedures.

Reporting

SGO’s Approval to Operate a Pollutant Control Facilities (SK MOE Approval No. PO19-193) specifies reporting requirements.

Quarterly Report

A quarterly report will be submitted to the Saskatchewan Ministry of Environment within 60 days following the quarter being reported. The report will include the results of sampling programs and inspections.

Annual Environmental Report

An annual environmental report will be submitted no later than March 31 of the following year. The report should include the following information:

  • Data from the environmental monitoring programs, with summaries and interpretation of data
  • Performance with respect to discharge limits for released effluent
  • Quality assurance and quality control program for field and laboratory procedures
  • Status of the TMFs, including volumes of tailings deposited and remaining capacity
