Document Control

Objectives

This manual is considered a “living document” and is regularly updated to

  • keep its contents current in consideration of mine development changes affecting tailings management and
  • provide a regular opportunity to review its accuracy and completeness.

Updates to This Manual

The following changes may trigger an update to this manual:

  • Substantial variation of observed tailings deposition performance from predicted performance
  • Recommendations by the EORengineer of record or any other qualified geotechnical professional who undertook an audit or safety review of the facilities
  • Changes in ownership, organizational structure, and/or individual responsibilities
  • Changes in the status of the mine such as expansion of facilities or operations, transition from construction to operation, or from temporary or permanent closure (and ultimately post closure)
  • Regulatory and/or operational permit changes including updated best practice guidelines for mine waste management
  • Changes in the operational reference documents or to their storage locations

Frequency of Updates

This manual is reviewed and updated as required, but at least once per year.

Update Procedure

Updates are executed in the following manner:

Step

Action

1

Submit proposed changes to the SGOSeabee Gold Operations Responsible Person for approval.

2

Submit approved changes related to design elements (Site Description and Tailings Facility Description) to the EORengineer of record.

3

Update manual with approved changes.

Note: Updates should be implemented once approved and the manual updated.
