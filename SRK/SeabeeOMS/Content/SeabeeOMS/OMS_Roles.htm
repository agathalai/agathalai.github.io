Overview

This section lists contacts and their roles and responsibilities related to the management of the tailings facilities at SGO and the procedures for managing their changes.

  • the operation, management, surveillance, and emergency preparedness and response of tailings management;

Complete definition of the Accountable Executive Officer, Responsible Person, and the EOR/Deputy EOR are provided in the SGO/EOR Agreement and in Developing an Operation, Maintenance and Surveillance Manual for Tailings and Water Management Facilities (MAC 2019b).

Future versions of this manual will be updated with the normal changes in staffing, roles, and responsibilities that occur throughout the life of a mining operation.
