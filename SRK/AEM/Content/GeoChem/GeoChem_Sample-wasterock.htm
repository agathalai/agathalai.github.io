Waste Rock

The GMP should provide guidance on site specific waste rock management and sampling. However, in all cases routine sampling and analysis of waste rock before it is placed in the long term storage area will significantly reduce environmental risk and closure planning uncertainty for a minimal cost (the analytical costs per sample are typically around $6 to $10). Table 1 provides some general guidance.
